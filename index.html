核查结果与处理措施
Outcome After Review
核查属实：发出《履约敦促函》
Verified: Issue a Performance Reminder Letter
向相关分包商正式发出《履约敦促函》。
A formal Performance Reminder Letter will be issued to the relevant subcontractor.
要求在规定期限内提交回复、完成整改并接受后续核查。
The subcontractor must respond, complete rectification and accept follow-up review within the required period.
整改完成并核实后，以书面方式告知投诉人处理结论。
After rectification and verification, the complainant will receive written feedback.
核查不成立：发出《不予受理告知书》
Not Verified: Issue a Notice of Non‑Acceptance of Complaint
书面说明不予受理或证据不足的原因。
Written explanation will be given for non-acceptance or insufficient evidence.
如投诉人提供新的补充证据，我司将重新启动核查程序。
If new supporting evidence is provided, the review process may be reopened.
如对调查结果不接受，建议依据相关合同约定寻求法律途径解决。
If the investigation result is not accepted, the parties are advised to seek legal remedies in accordance with the relevant contract.
重要提示：CHEC受理投诉并不意味着对争议事实或付款责任作出预先承认。
Important Notice: Acceptance of a complaint by CHEC does not constitute any prior admission of facts in dispute or payment liability.
